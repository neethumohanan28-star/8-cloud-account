Steps to create Microsoft Azure Account

Cloud Provider: Microsoft Azure

Step-by-Step Account Creation Process:

Step 1: Opened a web browser and visited the official Microsoft Azure website.
Step 2: Clicked on the "Start free" or "Free account" option.
Step 3: Signed in using an existing Microsoft account. (If not available, create a new one using an email address).
Step 4: Verified the email address by entering the verification code sent to the email.
Step 5: Entered personal details such as name, country/region, and phone number.
Step 6: Completed phone verification by entering the OTP received on the registered mobile number.
Step 7: Added a credit card details for identity verification.
Step 8: Confirmed the payment details (no charges were made during signup).
Step 9: Accepted the terms and conditions.
Successfully completed the registration and accessed the Azure Portal dashboard

Verification Performed:

> Email verification: Yes
> Phone verification: Yes (OTP-based)

Payment Method Required:

> Yes (Credit/Debit card required for verification only)

Notes:

1. Azure provides $200 free credits valid for 30 days.
2. Selected services are available free for 12 months with usage limits.
3. Charges apply only if free limits are exceeded.
